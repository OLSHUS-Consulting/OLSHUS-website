OLSHUS is a Queensland-based data consultancy founded by Sunny Hussey-Smith - a data and analytics engineer with over a decade of experience turning data into insights and solutions, and a Masters in Data Science to back up the wizard hat.

We specialise in cloud data warehousing, data modelling, engineering and AI - implementing a best-of-breed analytics stack and turning it into insights your business can act on.

We're a small team of trusted consultants who work best with organisations that genuinely value their data and want to unlock what's in it - collaboratively, pragmatically, and with the occasional flourish of wizardry.
